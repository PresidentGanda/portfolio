Hydration Station:

The Hydration Station is my remote plant control app that senses the hydration levels of the soil around your plant. This app allows the user to see whether their plant is dry even when they aren't home and has a feature that moves the stopper for a set amount of time and releases water into pot. I made this app and prototype with the help of a partner on particle IDE and VS code.

Thumper Jumper:

Thumper Jumper is a vertical parkour game that revolves around Bob, a fly with his wings broken. One morning, he's late for work at the fly factory and decides to take a shortcut up to his floor in order to try and get there before his boss notices. On his way up he encounters frogs that stop him from going up and when he reaches the top Boss Man tells him to get to work. I also made the code for this game mostly with Java Script but I also used HTML and CSS.

Choose Your Own Adventure:

This choose your own adventure is about waking up on a wednesday morning with a weird feeling in your stomach. The options either lead you to going to school or staying up. In the end the ceiling in your room collapses, and if you decide to stay in your bed you get hit. I made this code with a lot of HTML and Java Script to determine what text pops up when you click the buttons.

Buzz Feed Quiz:

This quiz uses a combination of questions to determine which sith lord the user is the most like them. Each question is weighted differently depending on what answer is chosen. At the end it adds up the total count for each and determines which option has the largest sum to reveal the image of the character that fits the user.
